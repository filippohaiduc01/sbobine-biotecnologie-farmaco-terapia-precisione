English version
A collection of lecture transcripts and study notes ("sbobine") from courses in the Biotecnologie del Farmaco master's program, Farmaci Biotecnologici nella Terapia di Precisione track — Università degli Studi di Milano.

How these notes are made
Lectures are recorded and transcribed using WhisperX, then reorganized into structured study documents (PDF/DOCX) with the help of an AI assistant (Claude, Anthropic) — including outlines, thematic sections, and occasionally exam-style Q&A.
Important: these documents are personal reworkings, not verbatim transcriptions of the instructor's slides. The scientific content reflects what was taught in class, but the form is an original synthesis.

Disclaimer
This material is shared for personal and collective study purposes, non-commercially.
It does not replace attending lectures or the official material provided by instructors.
If an instructor requests removal of content related to their course, this will be done promptly — open an issue or contact the maintainer.
Transcription or interpretation errors are possible: always cross-check with official sources (textbooks, course slides, cited papers).

Repository structure
See the Italian section above — the folder structure is language-agnostic.

Project status
Currently maintained by a single student. External pull requests are not yet accepted, but this may change in the future — feel free to open an issue with suggestions or corrections.
